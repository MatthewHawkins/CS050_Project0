Prevent the Spread


COVID-19 is one of the most aggressively infectious diseases humanity has experienced so far. With over half a million cases reported as of March 26, 2020, it is important that everyone do their part in preventing the spread of this disease.



First, it's important to recognize the symptoms of COVID-19, and the difference between these symptoms, and the symptoms of other diseases, such as the flu or the common cold:


COVID-19 The Flu The Common Cold
Fever Fever Mild or No fever
Occasional Fatigue Fatigue Occasional Fatigue
Infrequent or No Sneezing Infrequent or No Sneezing Sneezing
Occasional Body Aches Body Aches Body Aches
Severe Dry cough Dry Cough Mild Cough
Difficulty Breathing Difficulty Breathing in Severe Cases No Difficulty Breathing
Shortness of Breath Rare Shortness of Breath No Shortness of Breath


According to the above symptoms, if you believe that you are infected with COVID-19, it is imperative that you do not spread the infection to others. Here are some tips to ensure that you do not get others sick:


  • Stay Home -
    It's important to ensure that, regardless of symptoms, you are limiting exposure to others whenever possible
  • Wash Your Hands -
    Ensure that you're washing your hands often, and for at least 20 seconds. Soap will cause the fat layer encasing the virus to break down, killing it
  • Work From Home -
    If your job allows, try to perform any work-related tasks from your home. This will limit your risk of exposure from co-workers, and help contain the spread of COVID-19
  • Wipe Down Household Surfaces -
    Use household cleaners to keep common surfaces, such as doorknobs, counters, keyboards, remotes, and other surfaces from hosting any germs, bacteria or viruses.
