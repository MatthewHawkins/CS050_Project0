Prevent the Spread


COVID-19 is one of the most aggressively infectious diseases humanity has experienced so far. With over half a million cases reported as of March 26, 2020, it is important that everyone do their part in preventing the spread of this disease.



First, it's important to recognize the symptoms of COVID-19, and the difference between these symptoms, and the symptoms of other diseases, such as the flu or the common cold:


COVID-19 The Flu The Common Cold
Fever Fever Mild or No fever
Occasional Fatigue Fatigue Occasional Fatigue
Infrequent or No Sneezing Infrequent or No Sneezing Sneezing
Occasional Body Aches Body Aches Body Aches
Severe Dry cough Dry Cough Mild Cough
Difficulty Breathing Difficulty Breathing in Severe Cases No Difficulty Breathing
Shortness of Breath Rare Shortness of Breath No Shortness of Breath
